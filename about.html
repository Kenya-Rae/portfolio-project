About Me

Hi! I’m Kenya-Rae — a creative soul with a love for all things tech, gaming, and food. I recently completed a Level 5 Diploma in Full Stack Web Development, and I'm passionate about building clean, user-friendly websites while still learning and growing every day as a developer.

Outside of coding, you’ll usually find me gaming on my PS5, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or editing content for my Instagram and gaming page. I love working with visuals, whether it's creating short video edits, baking something camera-ready, or styling a clean website layout.

I enjoy learning new tools, taking on fresh challenges, and finding ways to turn ideas into something real and useful. Whether it’s a website, a recipe, or a video clip, I’m always thinking: “how can I make this better, clearer, or more fun?”

Thanks for stopping by — feel free to explore my site or get in touch!
