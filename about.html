About Me

Hi! I’m Kenya-Rae — a creative soul with a love for all things tech, gaming, and food. I recently completed a Level 5 Diploma in Full Stack Web Development, and I'm passionate about crafting clean, user-friendly websites while continuing to learn and grow every day as a developer.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me gaming on my PS5, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or editing content for my Instagram and gaming page. I love working with visuals — whether it's creating short video edits, baking something camera-ready, or styling a clean site layout.

I’m always curious about new tools, eager to take on fresh challenges, and driven by the question: “how can I make this better, clearer, or more fun?” Whether it’s a website, a recipe, or a video clip, I’m all about turning ideas into something real and useful.

Thanks for stopping by — feel free to explore my site or reach out!
